## Tips for New Grads

- **Apply early.** Many new grad pipelines close months before the start date, especially at large tech companies.
- **Don't skip the referral.** A referral from a current employee significantly increases resume screening odds at most companies.
- **Check graduation eligibility.** Most "new grad" roles require graduation between a specific date range (e.g., Dec 2026 – Jun 2027). Read the requirements carefully.
- **Negotiate your offer.** Use [Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/) to benchmark offers before accepting.
- **Track your applications.** Use a spreadsheet or [Simplify](https://simplify.jobs/) to keep track of where you've applied.
